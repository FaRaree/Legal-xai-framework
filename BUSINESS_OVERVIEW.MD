## Commercialization & Technology Transfer

This framework is designed for commercialization in regulated, high-stakes domains including:

- Judicial decision support
- Legal compliance systems
- Risk assessment & auditing
- Government and institutional AI oversight

The core contribution — narrative translation of explainable AI outputs — represents a defensible layer positioned between statistical models and legal reasoning.

Commercial pathways include:
- Enterprise licensing
- API-based deployment
- Government and court systems
- University technology transfer and spin-out
